A collection of Rayne’s custom MMD outfit models

Welcome to my little corner — a digital closet where Rayne tries on every version of herself. Each outfit here is a snapshot of mood, color, and imagination. From casual uniforms to festival dresses, every model was made with care and a bit of personality.

I started this project simply to have fun experimenting with MMD models and costume design. Over time, it became a way to express how a single character can transform endlessly while still feeling like “her.”

ABOUT

My Character’s Closet is a small MMD outfit showcase built to archive Rayne’s costume variations and creative experiments.
